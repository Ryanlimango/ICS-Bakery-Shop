About Us

A small-owned business called "ICS Bakery Shop" specializes in homemade breads, pastry, cakes and coffee. We source the ingredients from local farmers and use traditional baking methods passed down through generations. We offer a wide variety of breads, including sourdough, whole wheat, and rye, as well as sweet treats They also offer custom cakes for special occasions, like birthdays and weddings. Our bakery has a cozy, rustic atmosphere and a friendly staff who are happy to chat with customers and make recommendations beside from that we promise to all our customers that all of our products that our made is fresh and tasty, so you can enjoy them straight from the oven.

ENSAYMADA


Our ensaymada is a sweet, fluffy pastry and this is homemade, The Ensaymada is originating from the Philippines. It is traditionally made with butter, sugar, and flour, and topped with cheese. Some variations include the addition of milk, eggs, and yeast to give it a more distinctive texture and flavor.
